Week 4: The Parable of the Talents

Pastor Kael January 26, 2026 38 min
Scripture
Matthew 25:14-30
About This Message
In the Parable of the Talents, Jesus teaches us about faithfulness with what God has entrusted to us. Whether we have much or little, we are called to steward our gifts, time, and resources for His kingdom purposes.

This week we explore what it means to be faithful stewards and how we can multiply what God has given us for His glory.
Discussion Questions
1. What talents has God given you?
Think about your skills, resources, relationships, and time.
2. What does faithful stewardship look like in your daily life?
How can you invest what God has given you?
3. What fears hold you back from using your gifts?
The servant who buried his talent was afraid. What about you?
